About PageTurner

PageTurner was born as a fictional project with a real purpose: to show how technology, design, and literature can meet to create unique experiences. Created by enthusiasts of data, reading, and web development, our digital bookstore is a space for learning, practice, and innovation.

Here, you can explore books, authors, and categories, and use a full RESTful API built with Django + Ninja. Our platform serves both as a showcase for works and as a database for technical studies and demonstrations.
